Dog

Dogs like to run, sleep and stay happy.

    Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 30 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Home or kennel

Dogs are fun to be around. They make people smile. They're a little messy because they start licking you all over but hey - that's what makes us smile. Dogs are very loyal. I watched a video where a dog protected a young girl's life against an assailant. Very touching.
